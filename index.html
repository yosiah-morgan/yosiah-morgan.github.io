Josiah Morgan is a Junior studying Emerging Media Arts at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln. Josiah specializes in experience design for digital media including, web and XR. Josiah has experience working for the Perceptual Technologies Laboratory at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln where he helped produce New Kind of Weather, No 1, a dance installation. Josiah will be graduating in the Spring of 2023 with a BFA in Emerging Media Arts.
